About FilmNation

Film Nation was thought of and created in 2019 by CodeNation students Vaughn G. and Nathan A. It took weeks to perfect and tweak to be as functional and appealing as it is today! Using API reuqests and technology, along with basic web design skills, FilmNation instantaneously searches the web for information on your favorite movies.
